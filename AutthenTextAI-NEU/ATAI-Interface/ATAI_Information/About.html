Using AuthenText AI is simple yet powerful. Upon submission,
our algorithm meticulously analyzes the text, comparing it
against a vast database of known AI-generated content and
human-authored works. Through a combination of linguistic
analysis, pattern recognition, and machine learning, AuthenText
AI accurately identifies any instances of AI-generated text,
preserving the integrity of academic assessments.

Implementing AuthenText AI offers numerous benefits for educational institutions, students, and educators alike. By maintaining academic integrity, AuthenText AI safeguards the credibility of academic assessments and diplomas, instilling confidence in the educational process. For students, AuthenText AI promotes original thinking and discourages unethical behavior, fostering a culture of intellectual honesty. Educators benefit from streamlined assessment processes and actionable insights into student performance, enabling personalized feedback and targeted interventions.

Our AI detection model includes several components that analyze text
to determine its origin and if it was written by AI. We use a multi-stage
methodology designed to optimize accuracy while minimizing false
positives and negatives. From the macro level to the micro one, this is
how DeepAnalyse™ Technology works. Our model specializes in
identifying AI generated content like Chat GPT, GPT 3, GPT 4, Bard,
LLaMa models … Finally, we employ a comprehensive deep learning
methodology, trained on extensive text collections from the internet,
educational datasets, and our proprietary synthetic AI datasets
produced using various language models.
